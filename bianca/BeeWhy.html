La Ruta del Burro:


Illustration

About the Project

This illustration project was made in collaboration with Vortic Studio for a San Diego-based Mexican food vendor named "La Ruta Del Burro".

The Process

I penciled in a couple of rough sketches that aluded to the Mexican food vendor team. The style was supposed to be simple yet still keep that traditional Mexican charm that defined the brand.

Then, I passed the sketches into digital so they could be used as vector art for the designers in charge of the company's branding.

Final Product

*All further designs past the illustrations were made by Vortic Studio.

Dermanelly:


Social Media Content

About the Project

Dermanelly is the social media account of Mexican Dermatologist Dr. Nelly Espinoza of Monterrey, where she posts informative content and advice to all of her avid followers. As an intern at Vortic Studio, I was tasked with the monthly creation of these posts.

The Process

There is a document with plans of post for the month, they contain copies of what the posts will say and specific design instructions. Once I have that as a base I start mapping out the posts and make sure they go in accordance to the brand’s aesthetic.

Final Product

Umai Sushi:


Social Media Content

About the Project

Umai Sushi is a Japanese restaurant located in Tijuana, Mexico. I was tasked with designing some of their social media content for their socials when I worked as an intern for Vortic Studio.

The Process

First I brainstormed a few ideas for what kind of stories the restaurant could post. The best ideas were chosen and I got on to sketching rough drafts.

The pictures were taken by Vortic Studio on location in Umai Sushi to later be added under the designs.
